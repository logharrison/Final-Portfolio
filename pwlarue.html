P.W. LaRue Co. Shop

A clean and minimal webshop with an emphasis on the products.

About P.W. LaRue Co.

A local Chicago craftsman, and close friend of mine, approached me a few years ago with the idea of launching on online storefront for his handmade leather and canvas goods. Excited at the prospect of a design/dev project so removed from the products we sell at SteelSeries, I set off to build him an e-commerce site worthy enough to represent his beautiful products.

For product pages, we wanted to put all the emphasis on the product itself and keep the design as clean and minimal as possible. Each color variant actually has it's own page, but is still accessible from its siblings.

The entire site/shop is a custom Wordpress theme built on Timber, so I could utilize TWIG for templating. For the ecommerce backend, the theme uses WooCommerce so we could avoid monthly fees like with other services. Finally, for the admin side, the theme uses Advanced Custom Fields.
